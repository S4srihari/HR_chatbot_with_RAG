 HR POLICY: EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY
PURPOSE
This policy defines the types of leave available to employees, eligibility, approval process, 
and guidelines for use of leave. The goal is to support employee well-being
while ensuring business continuity.

SCOPE
This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of the organization 
unless otherwise specified in an employment contract.

TYPES OF LEAVE

3.1 Casual / Personal Leave
Employees are entitled to casual leave for personal matters.
Entitlement: X days per year
Approval: Prior approval from the reporting manager
Unused casual leave may/may not be carried forward (as per company rule)

3.2 Sick Leave
Sick leave is provided for illness or medical reasons.
Entitlement: X days per year
Medical certificate required if leave exceeds X consecutive days
Unused sick leave may/may not be carried forward

3.3 Earned / Annual Leave
Earned leave is for vacation or extended personal time.
Entitlement: X days per year
Accrued monthly or annually
Can be carried forward up to X days
Encashment rules apply as per company policy

3.4 Maternity Leave
Provided to eligible female employees as per applicable labor laws.
Duration: As per government regulations
Must be applied in advance with medical documentation

3.5 Paternity Leave
Provided to male employees at the time of childbirth.
Entitlement: X days
Must be used within X days of the child's birth

3.6 Bereavement Leave
Granted in the event of the death of an immediate family member.
Entitlement: X days
No deduction from other leave types

3.7 Unpaid Leave / Leave Without Pay (LWP)
Granted when all paid leave is exhausted or in special cases.
Subject to management approval
May impact salary and benefits

LEAVE APPLICATION PROCESS
All leave requests must be submitted through the official HR system or in writing/email
Employees must apply in advance except in emergencies
Reporting manager and HR approval is required
Emergency leave must be informed as soon as possible

RESPONSIBILITIES

Employees:
Plan leave in advance
Ensure proper handover of work
Follow the leave approval process

Managers:
Review and approve leave fairly
Ensure business continuity
Communicate any constraints clearly

HR:
Maintain leave records
Ensure compliance with labor laws
Handle exceptions and disputes

MISUSE OF LEAVE
Misuse of leave, falsification of documents, or habitual absenteeism may result in disciplinary action.

POLICY REVIEW
This policy may be revised by management at any time based on organizational needs or
legal requirements.
